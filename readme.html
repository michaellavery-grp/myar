M.Y.A.R. — Mike's Yet Another Rogue

v1.0.0 — a classic terminal roguelike in the manner of Rogue (1980) and The Mines of Moria. Pure Python, pure curses, zero dependencies. See README.md for the full manual; this page is the color-rendered tour.

The Quest

The Amulet of Yendor lies 4,950 feet down on level 99, held by Morgoth, Lord of Darkness. A named boss guards every fifth level. Kill Morgoth, take the Amulet, climb home. Death is permanent.
